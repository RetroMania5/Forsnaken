GUIDE

CONTROLS
WASD / joystick — move
Shift / push joystick far — sprint (drains stamina)
E / tap FIX — attempt a skill check on a nearby generator (survivor)
Q / tap ATK — basic attack (killer)
Z, X, C / mobile ability buttons — fire your abilities (for killers Z is the main attack, X / C are the abilities)
VITALS
HP: 100 for survivors. Down at 0. Killers can't be damaged.
Stamina: 100 (survivors) / 150 (killers). Sprint drains 12/s, regens 18/s when not sprinting. Abilities cost no stamina — only their cooldown gates them.
Speed: killers are slightly faster than the fastest survivor — even Stalker just barely edges out Runner.
SKILL CHECKS
Stand near a generator and tap when the spinning pointer is in the wedge:
• Green: +22% progress
• Yellow: +10% progress
• Miss (red): −12% progress + brief cooldown
Engineers get +30% to all positive progress.
ROUND TIMER
Starts at 6:00, counts down. Survivors win at 0:00. Killer wins by downing every survivor first.
Generator completed: −20s (pulls the survivor win closer).
Survivor downed: +25s (gives killer more hunt time).

Runner+10% speed

X — Smoke Bomb: drop a 260-radius cloud, lasts 3.5s. Survivors inside it are invisible to the killer (the killer still sees the cloud). CD 20s.
Engineer+30% repair
Z — Overcharge: instantly +30% progress to the nearest unfinished generator within 90px. CD 15s.
X — Mend: heal +50 HP over 2s. CD 20s.
Scout+5% speed / +5% repair
Z — Rally: you and survivors within 200px get +25% speed for 4s. CD 14s.
X — Scan: reveals the killer with a red ring + a floating arrow above you pointing toward them (with distance) for 2s. CD 12s.
Frost Knightslows the killer (-5% repair)
Z — Stun Burst: instant aoe, killers within 180px are slowed to 50% speed for 10s. CD 12s.
X — Snow Field: 0.7s summoning channel (you're rooted), then a frost field drops where you stand, 220 radius. Killers inside it move at 60%, and the slow lingers ~5s after they leave. Lasts until the killer destroys it (or the round ends). Max 3 active at a time — placing a 4th pops the oldest. CD 18s.
Sniperstuns the killer (-2% speed / -5% repair)
Z — Shoot: tap once to aim (you crouch and can't move; the camera shifts). On desktop, the reticle follows your mouse; on mobile the joystick steers. Tap again to fire a bullet at the reticle. Hits the killer → 10s stun. The bullet breaks on the first wall or obstacle. Generous hit radius. Costs 1 ammo. CD 25s on fire.
X — Reload: 5s channel to refill your 1 round. Only usable when empty. CD 20s. While aiming, this button backs out — your shot is unspent, but Shoot goes on a short 5s cooldown.
C — Sneak: vanish for 6s — you're mostly invisible to everyone (the killer barely sees you). Great for repositioning after a missed shot. CD 20s.
Fencerbalanced, melee stun + self-heal soda
Z — Slash: short-reach sword swing (80 px). If a killer is in range they're stunned for 5s (no damage). CD 25s.
X — Soda: drinks a can — heals 30 HP over 10s. CD 30s. Only 3 uses per round (shown as dots on the ability slot).

TIPS
• Sprint is for emergencies — light walking lets you sneak around corners without flooring your stamina.
• Mobile: push the joystick almost all the way to sprint; light touches walk.
• Tap a skill check in the green wedge instead of the yellow — finishes generators much faster.
• Use Test mode to feel each character's abilities before joining a round.
